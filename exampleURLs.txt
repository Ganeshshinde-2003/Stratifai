## 💡 Tips for Best Results

✅ **Works Great:**
- Company homepages (stripe.com, notion.so)
- Product landing pages
- Simple, content-rich pages
- Pages with clear product descriptions

❌ **May Not Work Well:**
- JavaScript-heavy single-page apps
- Pages behind authentication
- Pages with bot protection (Cloudflare challenges)
- Very slow-loading pages (>10 second timeout)

## 📝 What to Expect

When you use a URL, the scraper will:
1. Fetch the page HTML
2. Extract title, meta description, headings, paragraphs
3. Clean the content (remove navigation, scripts, etc.)
4. Chunk to 4000 characters max
5. Send to the 3-step LLM pipeline

Good URLs = Better marketing strategies! 🚀
